# Contributing to BorgFS

Thank you for your interest in contributing to BorgFS!

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

To ensure that we can safely manage the project and maintain our dual-licensing model, all contributors must agree to the Contributor License Agreement (CLA). 

Please read the full text of the agreement in the `CLA.md` file located in the root directory of this repository.

### How it works

By contributing to this project, you agree to the terms of the CLA. Every commit must include a `Signed-off-by` line with your real name and email address to indicate your acceptance of the agreement.

For example, add this to your commit messages:
`Signed-off-by: John Doe <john.doe@example.com>`
